Description!

Egusi is a typical Igbo recipe. It is made of Egusi seed, Ukazi leaf and Palm Oil. I can gurantee you that a taste will convince you.

Ingredients!

  • Egusi
  • Ukasi
  • Red Oil

Steps to prepare a delicious Lasagna!

  1. Boil 1 Litre of water with red oil, meat, fish till the mixture is done
  2. Add the Egusi seed and leave it to boil for about 4mins
  3. Add Ukazi leave. After 3min the food is ready
