Rice Soup Recipe!

Description!

Rice soup is a typical Nigerian recipe. It is not only delicious, it is also reach in protein. I can gurantee you that a taste will convince you.

Ingredients!

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Groundnut Oil

Steps to prepare a delicious Rice Soup!

  1. Boil the rice for 15mins
  2. Grind the tomatoes with some pepper
  3. Prepare the stew and mix it with the boiled rice
