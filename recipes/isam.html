Isam Recipe

Description!

Isam is a typical South-south recipe. It is made of Isam, Vegetable and Palm Oil. I can gurantee you that a taste will convince you.

Ingredients!

  • Isam
  • Vegetable
  • Cow Meat

Steps to prepare a delicious Lasagna!

  1. Wash the Isam thoroughly to remove every dirt
  2. Cook it for 10mins.
  3. Mix all the ingredients and warm till mixture blends very well
